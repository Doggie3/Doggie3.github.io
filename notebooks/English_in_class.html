英语课内默写单词

latest:2024-09-25
  • abandon
  • absorb 专心致志的
    • Be ~ed in doing sth
    • be buried in doing sth
    • be hooked on doing
    • be addicted to (上瘾)
  • abstract
    • 1. adj. 抽象的
    • 2. n. 概要
  • academic 学术的
    • Raise (及物动词) your ~ performance 提升专业水平
      • Rise n. 兴起 with the rise of
      • Arise v. (不及物动词) 出现 A problem arises from
      • E.g. A problem arises from his lateness
      • Arouse v. 引发 arouse curiosity/heated discussion 引发激烈讨论
    • Perform v. 表现
      • Outperform 在表现上超过…
      • Outnumber 在数字方面超过…
  • access
    • 1. v. 到达 access someplace
    • 2. n. have access to someplace
    • Adj. 可达到的 accessible sth. is accessible to sb.
      • This island is accessible to the public
  • available
    • 琳琅满目:
      • A variety of goods
      • A wide range of
  • accommodate
    • n. 食宿
    • v. 容纳
  • account n. 描述, 称述, 解释
    • 1. Give me a brief account of sth 简要描述一下…
    • 2. Account for + num 占比
    • 3. On no account 绝不 On no account can you lend $...
    • 4. Take sth into account 把...考虑在内
    • 5. On account of 由于, 因为
  • accuse v. 指控
    • Accuse sb of sth. 指控某人 = charge sb with sth.
  • acknowledge
    • 1. v. 承认
    • 2. v. 感谢
    • 3. v. 告知✉️
  • adapt 适应
    • 1. adapt to
    • 2. adapt for 改变编
    • 3. adapt from 从…改编
    • 4. 区分 adopt 领养
  • add up to = amount v.
  • addict v.
    • 1. be addicted to doing
    • 2. be buried in doing
    • 3. be hooked on doing
    • 4. be absorbed in doing
    • n. 人
      • addict 瘾君子
      • advocate 倡议/倡议者
      • suspect 嫌疑犯
      • nebel 叛逆者
      • witness 目击证人
  • address
    • 1. address issue
    • 2. address the meeting 在大会上发表演讲
  • adequate
  • admit doing 承认做…confess
  • advantage
    • 1. take advantage of
    • 2. win an ~ over/form/off/beyond/with(out) sb.
  • affair 事务
    • Event 事件
    • Incident
  • affect v.
    • Affection 情感
  • against
  • agree with () / to (强迫) / on (协商) sb.
  • air
  • alert
  • allowance 钱
    • balance 余额
    • pension
    • grant
  • alternative 替换
  • amateur
  • ambition = aspiration
  • amount v. amount to
  • analyze analysis 分析单数
  • ancestor
  • annoy bore interest amaze frustrate upset 使某人…
  • ape n. 猿人 v. 模仿 = imitate = mimic
  • appeal v. 呼吁，吸引，上诉
  • approach
    • n. 方式，方法
    • v. 靠近 with exam approaching
    • As exam is approaching
  • approve v. 批准 approve of
    • n. approval opp. disapproval
    • Eg. -al arrive arrival
    • renew renewal 续期
  • architect 建筑师
  • arise 出现 arouse 引发 rise 兴起 raise 养
  • arm n. 军火，手臂
    • v. 装备 = be equipped with
  • arrange v. 安排
    • arrange sb to do sth.
    • appeal sb to do sth. 呼吁
  • aspect n. 方面 in all aspects
  • assemble n. 集合 assembly line 装配线
  • assess 评估
    • access 到 sth/sb is accessible to sb/sth = available
  • assume
    • 1. 假定 assumption/assume that …
    • 2. 承担 assume the responsibility
    • 3. 呈现 = take on
  • assure -> sb of
    • E.g. sb of
      • rob sb of
      • rid sb of
      • remind sb of
      • inform sb of
    • Ensure sb that 确保
    • Insure - insurance 保险
  • astronaut 宇航员
